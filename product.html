The hardware and the app

One band. Six sensors. Zero spreadsheets.

PulseBand pairs a medical-grade sensor stack with software that does the interpreting for you. Here's everything inside.

Optical heart rate

Green-and-infrared LED array samples every 0.3 seconds, holding accuracy through sprints, lifts and long runs alike.

Cuffless blood pressure

Pulse-transit-time estimation gives you a systolic/diastolic read without ever reaching for a cuff.

Sleep-stage tracking

Accelerometer and heart-rate variability combine to separate light, deep and REM sleep, hour by hour.

Hydration modeling

Sweat-rate estimates from skin conductance plus your logged intake trigger a nudge before you feel thirsty.

On-device AI insights

A small on-band model turns raw sensor data into one plain-language sentence, updated after every activity.

Water resistant to 50m

Swim, shower or get caught in the rain — PulseBand keeps recording without missing a beat.

Fit and finish

A band you'll actually keep wearing.

The strap is woven nylon over a soft silicone lining — breathable enough for a 90-minute run, low-profile enough to forget you're wearing it to bed. The display auto-dims below 2 lux so it never lights up a dark room.

  • 1.3″ AMOLED display, 450 nits
  • 11-day battery, 2-hour full charge
  • 18g, including strap
  • Bluetooth 5.3, iOS and Android
